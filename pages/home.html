How it works

You have built out a startup and started scaling or maybe you have an idea and want to present the idea to potential investors or clients or maybe just family & friends, but not too sure how to go about it?? You have come to the right place – we have the easiest business plan / pitch deck creator.

You start off by giving us some information on your company, and our intuitive creative engine asks you relevant information and using this our AI research engine will put out industry information. Once you sign off on all the information, our creative engine takes over, giving you multiple themes to select – each one customised for your specific industry. Once you feel the information accurately represents your business, you can download the pitch document or business plan and the financial model – yes, both historical financials (if any) and projections.
